About This Research

Understanding the 2026 Software Development Landscape

Research Initiative

The State of Software Development 2026 (SoSD 2026) is a comprehensive research initiative conducted by ECPI University to analyze and document the current state of the software development industry. This project aims to provide students, educators, and professionals with insights into emerging technologies, market trends, and best practices.

Research Objectives

Identify Trends

Track emerging technologies and methodologies shaping the software development industry in 2026.

Analyze Market Demand

Understand current hiring trends and skill requirements across different software development sectors.

Guide Learning Paths

Provide recommendations for students on which technologies and skills to prioritize in their education.

Share Best Practices

Document and share current industry best practices and standards for software development.

Methodology

This research combines multiple data sources including:

  • Industry surveys and interviews with software development professionals
  • Analysis of job market data and hiring trends
  • GitHub repository analysis and open-source community insights
  • Technology adoption metrics from industry reports
  • Academic research and publications in software engineering
  • Direct feedback from ECPI University students and alumni

Who Should Read This?

Students

Gain insights into which technologies and skills are most relevant for your software development career.

Educators

Understand market demands to better align curriculum with industry needs.

Professionals

Stay informed about industry trends and emerging opportunities in your field.

Employers

Understand the broader market context and talent landscape for your hiring strategy.
