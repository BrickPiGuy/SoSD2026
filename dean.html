Associate Dean's Corner

Insights and Perspectives on Software Development Education

Welcome

Welcome to the Associate Dean's Corner, where we share insights, perspectives, and reflections on the evolving landscape of software development education and industry practices. This section provides a space for discussion on how academic institutions can better prepare students for the challenges and opportunities in modern software development.

Featured Perspectives

Academic-Industry Partnership

Strengthening connections between universities and industry to ensure our curriculum remains relevant and our students are job-ready.

Emerging Skills Development

Identifying and integrating new technical and soft skills that are becoming essential in the modern software development field.

Student Success Pathways

Creating comprehensive support systems and learning pathways that enable students to achieve their academic and professional goals.

Latest Updates

Git, GitHub Desktop Implementation

The purpose of this project was to infuse Git, GitHub Desktop, and GitHub in all applicable Software Development courses. This allows for our students to passively create portfolios. The project is nearing completion, with 400-level courses currently being edited.

Curriculum Evolution in 2026

As we navigate the rapidly changing tech landscape, we're continuously updating our curriculum to reflect the latest industry standards. From artificial intelligence integration to cloud-native development, we ensure our students learn what matters most.

Building Tomorrow's Leaders

Beyond technical skills, we're committed to developing well-rounded professionals who can lead teams, communicate effectively, and make ethical decisions in their careers.

Industry Collaboration

Our partnerships with leading technology companies provide our students with real-world experience, internship opportunities, and mentorship that accelerates their professional growth.
